Director's Message

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the National Institute of Technology Raipur, an institution with a rich legacy and a steadfast commitment to excellence in technical education and research. Established in 1956 as Government Engineering College Raipur, with its foundation laid by the first President of India, Hon'ble Dr. Rajendra Prasad, and later inaugurated by the first Prime Minister, Hon'ble Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru, the institute has evolved into one of the premier institutions of national importance since becoming an NIT in 2005.
